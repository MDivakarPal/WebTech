Ingredients for Tea

  • Water
  • Tea Leaf
  • Sugar
  • Sugar Spices

Stetps to prepare

  1. Boil water. Different types of tea require different water temperature to brew.
  2. Warm up teapot.
  3. Put tea into teapot and add hot water.
  4. Cover teapot and steep tea.
  5. Strain tea solids and pour hot tea into tea cups
